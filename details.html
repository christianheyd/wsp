Mayones Regius 8

$1750.00

Manufacturer: Mayones
Type: Electric
Strings: 8
Scale Length: 29.5"
Color: Blue

When even 7 strings are not enough to set your creativity loose from the depth of mind, a Regius 8 is the solution. Whether you seek ambient clean passages high up the strings or djent power from down under Regius 8 lets you express what you hide deep inside. A Regius 8 sports a profiled Flamed Maple top on a Swamp Ash or optional Mahogany body and its highly recognizable 11-ply neck-thru laminate neck with baritone (687mm) scale. Comes with EMG 808 or Seymour Duncan Blackouts 8 pickups, Sperzel Trimlock tuners, ABM fixed bridge with strings-thru-body and Schaller Security Straplocks.
